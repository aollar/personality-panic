Six concentrated campaign moments

No daily content hamster wheel. Build a few strong assets, then distribute them during six deliberate moments.

01

Steam page reveal

Publish the page with a clear hook, readable capsule art, credible screenshots, and an initial gameplay-forward trailer.

02

Closed Playtest recruitment

Recruit friends, family, personality-community contacts, target gamers, and a few small creators into controlled testing waves.

03

Gameplay trailer reveal

Send the polished trailer to upcoming-game channels, indie showcase channels, personality creators, and gaming creators.

04

Public demo release

Turn the validated slice into a consumer-facing demo and give every outreach contact one clean call to action.

05

Steam Next Fest

Concentrate traffic on the public demo, host creator sessions, support players quickly, and measure wishlist conversion.

06

Launch-week coverage push

Re-contact warm creators, distribute release keys, activate Kendrick’s channels, and coordinate launch-day coverage.
