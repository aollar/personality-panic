Complete structure, reduced breadth

One map. One complete match. One strong reason to play again.

Keep all building categories required for the core loop, then reduce content depth elsewhere.

Go or no-go gates

Three decisions that prevent wishful launching

Gate 1 · August 31

Register for Next Fest?

  • Complete external matches are happening.
  • Core rules are no longer being reinvented.
  • Coming Soon page is public.
  • Demo can be completed in September.
Gate 2 · September 8

Stay in October?

  • 30+ unaffiliated testers.
  • Completion metrics trend toward targets.
  • At least half want another match.
  • Top complaints are polish, not core-loop failure.
Gate 3 · October 27

Launch November 17?

  • Full game is stable.
  • Demo and wishlist momentum are healthy.
  • Coverage is actually scheduled.
  • Release candidate can lock early.
  • Conservative sales case is acceptable.
