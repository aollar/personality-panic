Six concentrated campaign moments

Do not build a daily-content treadmill. Reuse a strong demo, trailer, Steam page, and creator pitch around six deliberate campaign windows.

01 Aug 3–16, 2026

Closed Playtest recruitment

Invite friends, family, personality-community contacts, and a small number of relevant gamers into the first controlled test waves.

02 Aug 24–31, 2026

Steam page reveal

Launch a polished Coming Soon page with a clear hook, strong screenshots, and the best gameplay-first trailer available.

03 September 7, 2026

Gameplay trailer reveal

Submit the trailer to upcoming-game channels, indie showcase channels, personality creators, and handpicked gaming creators.

04 October 8, 2026

Public demo release

Turn the validated Playtest build into the consumer-facing demo and begin the press-preview and creator-access window.

05 Oct 19–26, 2026

Steam Next Fest week

Concentrate all traffic on the public demo, host creator sessions, support coverage, and measure wishlist conversion.

06 Nov 10–17, 2026

Launch-week coverage push

Recontact warm creators, distribute launch keys, coordinate Kendrick’s channels, and activate the audience accumulated since August.
